About REUSE


REUSE is the center for reuse and circular economy in Århus.
At REUSE, you can hand in items and furnitures for others to pick up for free.
REUSE is a different kind of recycling station where bits and bobs get a second chance
instead of going to waste.
The free stuff you find at REUSE comes from you.
Hand in at REUSE
No matter where in the city of Århus you live, you can hand in useful objects at REUSE
during opening hours.
There is a short-term parking at REUSE.
Hand in for REUSE at the recylcing stations
You can find a special REUSE container at all six recycling stations in Århus.
The items you put in the containers will go to REUSE and be placed on the shelves here.
